<p><p>The Radio Pallet Shuttle System market can be categorized into two primary types: LIFO (Last In, First Out) and FIFO (First In, First Out) systems. LIFO systems prioritize the retrieval of the most recently stored pallets, making them ideal for products with a longer shelf life or seasonal items. In contrast, FIFO systems ensure that older stock is retrieved first, crucial for perishable goods or items with expiry dates, enhancing inventory turnover and reducing waste. Both systems optimize warehouse efficiency and space utilization.</p></p>
