Baskets, usually made of wicker or fiber composite, provide a safe space for passengers and pilots and house the burner system. Burners are crucial components that produce flame to heat the air in the envelope, directly influencing altitude and control. Each type plays a vital role in ensuring a successful and enjoyable ballooning experience.</p></p>
