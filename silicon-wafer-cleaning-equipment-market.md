<p><p>The silicon wafer cleaning equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: monolithic cleaning equipment and tank cleaning equipment. Monolithic cleaning equipment operates as a standalone unit, facilitating efficient, high-throughput cleaning of wafers with minimal footprint. In contrast, tank cleaning equipment utilizes large tanks filled with cleaning solutions, allowing for the immersion and rinsing of multiple wafers simultaneously. Both types are essential for ensuring the removal of contaminants, enhancing wafer quality, and supporting semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
