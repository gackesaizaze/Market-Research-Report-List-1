<p><strong>The Automotive VRLA Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AGM Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) battery market includes two primary types: AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries use glass mat separators to absorb electrolyte, providing high power output and faster recharging, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. Gel batteries, on the other hand, contain a silica-based gel that immobilizes the electrolyte, offering enhanced safety and vibration resistance. Both types are maintenance-free, reliable, and commonly used in applications requiring deep cycling and durability.</p></p>
