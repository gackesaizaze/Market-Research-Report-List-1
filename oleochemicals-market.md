<p><p>The oleochemicals market comprises several types including fatty acids, fatty alcohols, glycerin, and others. Fatty acids are carboxylic acids derived from fats and oils, widely used in soaps and detergents. Fatty alcohols, which are long-chain alcohols, are essential in cosmetics and surfactants. Glycerin, a versatile compound with moisturizing properties, finds applications in food, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products. Other oleochemicals include fatty esters and surfactants, utilized across various industries for their emulsifying and stabilizing properties.</p></p>
