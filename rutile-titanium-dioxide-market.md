<p><p>The Rutile Titanium Dioxide market comprises two primary production methods: the sulfate process and the chloride process. The sulfate process involves treating titanium ores with sulfuric acid, yielding high-grade titanium pigments but generating more waste. In contrast, the chloride process utilizes titanium tetrachloride and is considered more efficient, producing purer pigments with lower environmental impact. Both processes cater to different applications in coatings, plastics, and other industries, influencing their market dynamics and product characteristics.</p></p>
