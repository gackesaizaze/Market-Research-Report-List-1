<p><p>The veneers market primarily consists of two types: composite materials and dental porcelain. Composite veneers are made from a resin material, offering a more affordable and less invasive option for aesthetic improvements. They can be easily shaped and repaired but may have a shorter lifespan compared to porcelain. On the other hand, dental porcelain veneers are made from high-quality ceramic, providing a natural appearance and greater durability. These are ideal for long-lasting cosmetic solutions, although they require a more complex application process.</p></p>
