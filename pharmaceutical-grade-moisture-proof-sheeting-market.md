<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade moisture-proof sheeting market consists of various types of materials designed for packaging and protecting sensitive pharmaceuticals. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) sheets are popular for their durability and cost-effectiveness. PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) sheets offer excellent moisture barrier properties, making them ideal for high-quality packaging. PCTFE (Polychlorotrifluoroethylene) sheets are known for their exceptional chemical resistance and low permeability. PP (Polypropylene) sheets provide lightweight and recyclable options, catering to diverse pharmaceutical applications while ensuring product integrity against moisture.</p></p>
