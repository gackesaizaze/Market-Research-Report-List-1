<p><strong>The Manual Sphygmomanometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aneroid Sphygmomanometer</li><li>Mercury Sphygmomanometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual sphygmomanometer market comprises primarily two types: aneroid and mercury sphygmomanometers. Aneroid sphygmomanometers use a dial gauge to measure blood pressure and are portable and easy to use, making them popular in clinical settings. Mercury sphygmomanometers, which utilize a column of mercury to gauge pressure, offer high accuracy but are less favored due to safety concerns related to mercury disposal. Both types remain essential for precise blood pressure monitoring in various healthcare environments.</p></p>
