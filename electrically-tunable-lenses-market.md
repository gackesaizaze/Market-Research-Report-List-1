<p><p>Electrically tunable lenses are categorized into three main types: flat convex, flat concave, and flat designs. Flat convex lenses allow for positive optical power and are ideal for applications requiring magnification, while flat concave lenses provide negative optical power, suitable for scenarios like beam expansion. Flat designs offer unique advantages in compact systems, enabling versatile optical adjustments without significant bulk. These lenses are increasingly used in adaptive optics, imaging systems, and augmented reality, enhancing performance through adjustable focal lengths.</p></p>
