<p><strong>The Flexible AMOLED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible AMOLED display market consists primarily of Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) technologies. AMOLED displays utilize an active matrix of OLED pixels that provide higher resolutions, faster refresh rates, and better energy efficiency, making them suitable for smartphones and high-end displays. In contrast, PMOLED technology features a simpler design with a passive matrix for controlling the pixels, which is typically used in smaller screens like wearables and low-resolution applications.</p></p>
