<p><p>The autonomous shuttle sensors market comprises three primary types: camera sensors, radar sensors, and lidar sensors. Camera sensors capture visual information for object detection and recognition, crucial for navigation and safety. Radar sensors utilize radio waves to detect distance and speed, effective in various weather conditions. Lidar sensors employ laser beams to create detailed 3D maps of the environment, enhancing spatial awareness. Together, these sensor types enable safe and efficient operation of autonomous shuttles in urban settings.</p></p>
