<p><p>The Spring Applied Brakes market is primarily segmented into two types: Hydraulic Spring Applied Brakes and Electromagnetic Spring Applied Brakes. Hydraulic Spring Applied Brakes utilize hydraulic pressure to release the brakes when needed, providing reliable and consistent performance, especially in heavy machinery applications. Conversely, Electromagnetic Spring Applied Brakes use electromagnetic force to disengage the brakes, ensuring quick response times and ease of operation. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and efficiency in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
