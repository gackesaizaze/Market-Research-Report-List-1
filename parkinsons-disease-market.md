<p><p>The Parkinson's disease market comprises several treatment types. Carbidopa/Levodopa is the first-line therapy, converting to dopamine in the brain to alleviate symptoms. Dopamine receptor agonists mimic dopamine effects, offering benefits in early stages and reducing motor fluctuations. MAO-inhibitors block the breakdown of dopamine, enhancing its availability and providing a neuroprotective effect. Each treatment option serves to improve quality of life for patients by managing symptoms and addressing the progressive nature of the disease.</p></p>
