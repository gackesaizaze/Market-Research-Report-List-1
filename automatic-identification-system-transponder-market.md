<p><p>The Automatic Identification System (AIS) transponder market comprises two main types: Class A and Class B Marine AIS. Class A AIS transponders are primarily used by commercial vessels, providing real-time tracking and safety information to avoid collisions. They operate at higher power and transmit more detailed data. Class B AIS transponders are designed for smaller vessels, recreational boats, and hobbyists, offering basic tracking capabilities with lower power output and less frequent data transmission, enhancing situational awareness on the water.</p></p>
