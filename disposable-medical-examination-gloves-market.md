<p><p>The disposable medical examination gloves market consists of various types, each catering to different needs. Natural rubber gloves offer excellent dexterity and elasticity, making them popular for healthcare settings. Nitrile gloves are known for their puncture resistance and are suitable for those with latex allergies. Vinyl gloves provide a cost-effective option with lower durability, ideal for non-hazardous tasks. Neoprene gloves combine the benefits of both latex and nitrile, offering chemical resistance and a high level of comfort for medical professionals.</p></p>
