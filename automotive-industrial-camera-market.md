<p><p>The automotive industrial camera market includes various types, notably area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture complete images of a scene in one shot, making them ideal for applications like quality control and inspection in automotive manufacturing. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, which is useful for high-speed production lines where continuous monitoring is required. Other camera types may include stereo vision and thermal cameras, providing additional functionality for diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
