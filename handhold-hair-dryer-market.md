<p><strong>The Handhold Hair Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Hair Dryer</li><li>Axial Hair Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld hair dryer market includes two primary types: centrifugal and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers utilize a fan to draw air in and push it out at high speed, providing powerful airflow that dries hair quickly. In contrast, axial hair dryers feature a fan that directs air in a straight line, producing a gentler and more focused airflow. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, with centrifugal models often favored for speed, while axial types are appreciated for their precision and gentleness.</p></p>
