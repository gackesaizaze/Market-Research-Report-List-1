<p><p>The Bismaleimide (BMI) prepreg market comprises two main types: unidirectional and fabric BMI prepregs. Unidirectional BMI prepregs feature fibers oriented in a single direction, delivering high strength and stiffness, ideal for applications requiring load-bearing structures in aerospace and automotive industries. In contrast, fabric BMI prepregs consist of woven fibers, providing multidirectional strength and flexibility, making them suitable for complex shapes and designs. Both types are essential in producing lightweight, high-performance composite materials for advanced engineering applications.</p></p>
