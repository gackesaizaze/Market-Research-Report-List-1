<p><p>The electrostrictive stack actuator market can be divided into two main types: pre-stress electrostrictive stack actuators and no pre-stress electrostrictive stack actuators. Pre-stress actuators are designed to operate under a predetermined mechanical stress, enhancing their performance and stability, which makes them suitable for precise applications requiring controlled movement. No pre-stress actuators, on the other hand, operate without additional mechanical stress, offering flexibility but potentially lower efficiency. Both types serve various industries, including aerospace, robotics, and precision engineering.</p></p>
