<p><p>The Flex-Rigid PCB market encompasses three primary types: Single Layer, Double Layer, and Multi-Layer PCBs. Single Layer boards feature a single conductive layer and are ideal for simple applications. Double Layer boards incorporate two conductive layers, allowing for more complex designs while maintaining flexibility. Multi-Layer PCBs consist of multiple conductive layers, enabling advanced circuitry in compact spaces, making them suitable for high-density applications. Each type serves distinct needs in the electronics industry, catering to varying levels of complexity and functionality.</p></p>
