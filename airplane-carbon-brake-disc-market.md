<p><strong>The Airplane Carbon Brake Disc Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVD</li><li>Short Fiber Impregnated Carbonization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airplane Carbon Brake Disc market is primarily categorized into CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) and Short Fiber Impregnated Carbonization types. CVD brake discs are created by depositing carbon layers onto a substrate, resulting in high strength and durability, ideal for high-performance aircraft. In contrast, Short Fiber Impregnated Carbonization brake discs utilize a matrix of short carbon fibers mixed with resin, enhancing thermal stability and weight efficiency. Both types are crucial for improving braking efficiency and safety in aviation.</p></p>
