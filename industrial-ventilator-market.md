<p><p>The industrial ventilator market is categorized into three main types: Inhalation, Exclusion, and Combined ventilators. Inhalation ventilators focus on delivering fresh air to workers in hazardous environments. Exclusion ventilators aim to remove or dilute harmful contaminants, ensuring a safer atmosphere. Combined ventilators integrate both functionalities, providing comprehensive air management by supplying clean air while simultaneously displacing pollutants. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining air quality and protecting worker health in various industrial settings.</p></p>
