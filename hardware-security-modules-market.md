<p><p>Hardware Security Modules (HSMs) come in various types, each catering to different infrastructure needs. LAN-based HSMs connect over a network, enabling centralized security for multiple users and systems, ideal for large organizations. PCIe-based HSMs are integrated directly into servers, providing high-speed, low-latency cryptographic functions for demanding applications. USB-based HSMs offer portability and ease of use, suitable for smaller setups or individual users requiring secure cryptographic key management without extensive infrastructure. Each type balances security, performance, and convenience to meet diverse requirements.</p></p>
